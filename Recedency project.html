Resedency

My regular visit if I am in Chennai. Samosas, Jilebi and lassi are my pick. Very busy at most times, but no compromise on quality or taste. Very economical and just tasty food. You can be sure of food hygiene.Below Average...Nothing special except for the leaf 'plates'. Overall place lacks hygiene ! There are thoughtless gang of foodies going around who are unoriginal and simply make yet another video on what someone before them did. This 'Bombay Lassi' is a beneficiary of this trend. The samosas at 'Bombay Lassi' are nothing special - except for their biodegradable leaf packing. If you do a blind taste test, you can get much better samosas in most restaurants - although price wise these samosas are cheaper. Their Lassi is nothing to write home about either. Overall - one hopes these 'foodies' promote truly quality places - who combine quality, value and hygiene
